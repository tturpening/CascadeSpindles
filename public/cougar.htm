Comprised of 3,082 acres with more than 36 miles of trails, Cougar Mountain is surrounded by the cities of Bellevue, Newcastle and Issaquah, and just minutes from downtown Seattle. Equally impressive is the scenery along the way and the interesting destinations that the trails lead to. Among the trail destinations are: a Nike missile site left overfrom the Cold War, coal mining shafts, spectacular waterfalls, a restored meadow that was once a baseball field in the 1920's, a former mining town site, large glacier boulders, caves and mountain top view points.
