A hefty 2,000 foot gain to the summit, Tiger is located just off Interstate 90 near Issaquah, WA. Tiger is a popular hiking area so close to civilization that it almost feels like cheating to hike there and to find mountain wilderness so close to home. There is a vast menu of hikes to choose from; a hike for everybody….young or old, experienced or new. Tiger Mountain is an excellent place to watch the changing seasons; in spring it is rich with new growth and wild flowers. Tiger Mountain is a place of changing seasons and moods. It is a place to meditate and to dream, to socialize and celebrate life in all its' seasons.
